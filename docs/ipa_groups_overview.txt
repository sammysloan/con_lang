IPA Keyword Groups — Overview

Nasals
  What it holds: Nasal stops (airflow through the nose), across places of articulation.
  Count: 15
  Examples: m̥, m, ɱ̊, ɱ, n̼, n̥, n, ɳ̊ ...


Plosives
  What it holds: Oral stops (complete closure, burst release), voiced and voiceless.
  Count: 20
  Examples: p, b, p̪, b̪, t̼, d̼, t̪, d̪ ...


SibilantAffricates
  What it holds: Affricates with sibilant frication (ts, tʃ, tɕ, etc.).
  Count: 10
  Examples: t̪s̪, d̪z̪, ts, dz, t̠ʃ, d̠ʒ, tʂ, dʐ ...


NonSibilantAffricates
  What it holds: Affricates with non‑sibilant frication (pf, tθ, cç, etc.).
  Count: 19
  Examples: pɸ, bβ, p̪f, b̪v, t̪θ, d̪ð, tɹ̝̊, dɹ̝ ...


SibilantFricatives
  What it holds: Fricatives with grooved, high‑amplitude hiss (s, ʃ, ɕ, etc.).
  Count: 8
  Examples: s, z, ʃ, ʒ, ʂ, ʐ, ɕ, ʑ


NonSibilantFricatives
  What it holds: Other fricatives (ɸ β f v θ ð x ɣ χ ʁ ħ ʕ h ɦ, etc.).
  Count: 24
  Examples: ɸ, β, f, v, θ̼, ð̼, θ, ð ...


Approximants
  What it holds: Approximant consonants (ɹ, j, ʋ, ɰ, etc.).
  Count: 9
  Examples: β̞, ʋ, ð̞, ɹ, ɹ̠, ɻ, j, ɰ ...


Taps
  What it holds: Very brief single‑contact rhotics (ɾ, ɽ, etc.).
  Count: 9
  Examples: ⱱ̟, ⱱ, ɾ̼, ɾ̥, ɾ, ɽ̊, ɽ, ɢ̆ ...


Trills
  What it holds: Vibrant rhotics and related trills (r, ʀ, ʙ, ʜ/ʢ).
  Count: 11
  Examples: ʙ̥, ʙ, r̥, r, r̠, ɽ̊r̥, ɽr, ʀ̥ ...


LateralAffricates
  What it holds: Affricates with lateral release (tɬ, dɮ, etc.).
  Count: 8
  Examples: tɬ, dɮ, tꞎ, d𝼅, c𝼆, ɟʎ̝, k𝼄, ɡʟ̝


LateralFricatives
  What it holds: Lateral fricatives (ɬ, ɮ, etc.).
  Count: 8
  Examples: ɬ, ɮ, ꞎ, 𝼅, 𝼆, ʎ̝, 𝼄, ʟ̝


LateralApproximants
  What it holds: Lateral approximants (l series, ʎ, ʟ…).
  Count: 7
  Examples: l̪, l, l̠, ɭ, ʎ, ʟ, ʟ̠


LateralTaps
  What it holds: Taps with lateral articulation (ɺ, ʎ̆, ʟ̆…).
  Count: 6
  Examples: ɺ̥, ɺ, 𝼈̊, 𝼈, ʎ̆, ʟ̆


Bilabial
  What it holds: Bilabial consonants (both lips).
  Count: 12
  Examples: m̥, m, p, b, pɸ, bβ, ɸ, β ...


Labiodental
  What it holds: Labiodental consonants (lip + teeth).
  Count: 8
  Examples: ɱ̊, ɱ, p̪, b̪, f, v, ʋ, ⱱ


Linguolabial
  What it holds: Tongue‑to‑upper‑lip articulations.
  Count: 6
  Examples: n̼, t̼, d̼, θ̼, ð̼, ɾ̼


Dental
  What it holds: Dental consonants (tongue to teeth).
  Count: 10
  Examples: t̪, d̪, t̪s̪, d̪z̪, t̪θ, d̪ð, θ, ð ...


Alveolar
  What it holds: Alveolar articulations (tongue to alveolar ridge).
  Count: 24
  Examples: n̥, n, t, d, ts, dz, tɹ̝̊, dɹ̝ ...


PostAlveolar
  What it holds: Postalveolar consonants.
  Count: 11
  Examples: t̠ʃ, d̠ʒ, t̠ɹ̠̊˔, d̠ɹ̠˔, ʃ, ʒ, ɹ̠̊˔, ɹ̠˔ ...


Retroflex

  Count: 22
  Examples: ɳ̊, ɳ, ʈ, ɖ, tʂ, dʐ, ʂ, ʐ ...


Palatal
  What it holds: Palatal place of articulation.
  Count: 18
  Examples: ɲ̊, ɲ, c, ɟ, tɕ, dʑ, cç, ɟʝ ...


Velar
  What it holds: Velar place of articulation.
  Count: 15
  Examples: ŋ̊, ŋ, k, ɡ, kx, ɡɣ, x, ɣ ...


Uvular

Epiglottal
  What it holds: Epiglottal segments (including epiglottal fricatives/stops).
  Count: 8
  Examples: ʡ, ʡʜ, ʡʢ, ħ, ʕ, ʡ̆, ʜ, ʢ


Glottal

  Count: 5
  Examples: ʔ, ʔh, h, ɦ, ʔ̞


Obstruents
  What it holds: Stops, fricatives, and affricates (noise‑producing).
  Count: 81
  Examples: p, b, p̪, b̪, t̼, d̼, t̪, d̪ ...


VoicedObstruents
  What it holds: Subset of obstruents produced with vocal‑fold vibration.
  Count: 39
  Examples: b, b̪, d̼, d̪, d, ɖ, ɟ, ɡ ...


VoicelessObstruents
  What it holds: Subset of obstruents without vocal‑fold vibration.
  Count: 42
  Examples: p, p̪, t̼, t̪, t, ʈ, c, k ...


Sonorants
  What it holds: Nasals, approximants, liquids, taps, trills (generally voiced).
  Count: 73
  Examples: m̥, m, ɱ̊, ɱ, n̼, n̥, n, ɳ̊ ...


VoicedSonorants
  What it holds: Sonorants with voicing explicitly marked.
  Count: 50
  Examples: m, ɱ, n̼, n, ɳ, ɲ, ŋ, ɴ ...


VoicelessSonorants
  What it holds: Sonorants produced without voicing.
  Count: 24
  Examples: m̥, ɱ̊, n̥, ɳ̊, ɲ̊, ŋ̊, ɴ̥, ɾ̥ ...


CloseVowels

NearCloseVowels
